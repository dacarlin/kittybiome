We love cats

We are studying how microbes interact with cat biology, behavior and health. We are conducting the world's largest study of the microbiome of kitties.

We love science

With your help, we will create the world's largest database of cat microbiome data. We will openly share the results of this study with you. We invite everyone who cares about kitties to join this project.
